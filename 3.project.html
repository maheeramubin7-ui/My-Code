Solar System

The solar system consists of the Sun and all the celestial bodies that revolve around it. The sunis at the centre of the solar system and is the main source of light and heat. There are a total of eight planets. This planets move around the sun in fixed paths called orbits.

Besides planets, the solar system also includes moons, asteriods, comets and meteriods. The strong gravatational force of the Sun keeps all these objects bound togrther, making the solar system an important part of our universe.
